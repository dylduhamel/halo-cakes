About HALO

The Story Behind HALO Cakes

Hello! I'm Hailey Olivia Duhamel, the passionate baker behind HALO Cakes. My journey began in my grandmother's kitchen, where I learned that the secret ingredient in any recipe is love.

After culinary school and years working in top bakeries, I decided to bring my unique cake designs and flavors directly to clients who want something truly special for their celebrations.

At HALO Cakes, I believe every occasion deserves a cake that not only looks stunning but tastes incredible too. Each creation is handcrafted with premium ingredients and careful attention to detail, ensuring your cake is as unique as your celebration.

Our Cake Offerings

Delicious Flavors

Choose from our signature flavors including vanilla bean, rich chocolate, red velvet, lemon, carrot, coconut, and seasonal specialties. All cakes can be paired with complementary fillings and frostings.

Custom Designs

From elegant wedding cakes to whimsical birthday creations, each HALO cake is custom designed to match your vision. We specialize in buttercream artistry, fondant decorations, and edible printing.

Special Occasions

Celebrate life's milestones with a cake that's as special as the occasion. We create stunning cakes for weddings, birthdays, baby showers, anniversaries, graduations, and corporate events.
